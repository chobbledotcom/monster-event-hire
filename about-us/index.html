About us

Monster Event Hire has been established since 2002 by Joanne and David Morris.

We pride ourselves on safety, with our staff passing the RPII Training Award examination, adhering to the best practice set out in BS EN 14960:2019 and follow a strictly enforced code of conduct.  We now manufacture 90% of our inflatables and have our own RPII inspector on-site.

We are not an entertainment agency, all our equipment is our own, with our own full-time and in-house staff.

We carry £10 million Public Liability Insurance and all of our equipment carries either certified Pipa, RPII and PAT safety test certifications where applicable.

We guarantee to satisfy and exceed all your requirements. All this plus our valuable reputation for reliability, courtesy and service, will ensure your event is a great success and provide you with the best entertainment possible.

You may notice we do not display our prices, this is because the final prices depend on many variables, such as, travel, event staff, amount ordered, etc… please contact us for a quote!
