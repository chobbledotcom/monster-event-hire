Waterlooville is barely five miles up the A3 from the workshop. For most jobs there the kit is on site within twenty minutes of leaving the yard, which makes last-minute weekend bookings reasonable to pull off. We're in and out of PO7 and PO8 every week of the season for school summer fairs, scout fundraisers, weddings, and corporate days for the businesses around Denmead and Cowplain.

Because we're up that way most weeks anyway, same-day quotes are realistic and same-day deliveries are sometimes possible. The paperwork - insurance, risk assessment, PIPA - lives with the kit, so we can send it across without having to chase anyone.
