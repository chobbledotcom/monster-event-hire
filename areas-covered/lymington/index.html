Lymington sits about 30 miles west of our Havant base, on the south-eastern fringe of the New Forest. We deliver into SO41 most weekends through the summer — village fêtes, sailing-club family days, weddings at the country-house venues around Boldre and Sway, and the long list of community events across the marina town.

Coastal and exposed venues need a hire firm that takes wind monitoring seriously. Every inflatable hire from us is supplied with an anemometer and a documented wind-speed protocol — recordings every 30 minutes, mandatory evacuation at 24mph (Force 5). It's the HSE-aligned procedure and we don't shortcut it.
