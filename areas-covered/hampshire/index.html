Hampshire is our home county. We're based in Havant, with regular delivery runs every weekend from the M27 corridor across to Andover, Basingstoke, Winchester and the Test Valley. From the New Forest to the East Hampshire AONB, and from coastal Portsmouth out to the rural villages, we cover the entire county year-round.

Hampshire's councils, schools, weddings, charities and corporates have used Monster Event Hire since 2002. East Hampshire District Council have used us continuously since their community festival's first year. We also run our PIPA inspections and RPII operator training for other Hampshire hire companies, which means the standards we apply to our own fleet are the standards we audit other operators against across the county.
