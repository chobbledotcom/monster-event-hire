Farnborough is about 43 miles north of our Havant base — a routine run for us via the A3 and A331. We deliver across GU14 most weekends of the season, with a particularly strong corporate-event market around the business parks and Aerospace Centre, plus school fairs, scout group events and family parties across the town.

Larger corporate or public-facing events benefit from what we bring as standard: £10m Public Liability Insurance, PIPA-tagged inflatables, in-house RPII inspector, documented wind-safety protocol with anemometer, and DBS-checked operators on every booking. Insurance certificates and risk assessments are issued on request as part of the booking.
