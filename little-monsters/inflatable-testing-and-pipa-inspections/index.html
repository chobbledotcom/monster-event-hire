Inflatable Inspections & PIPA Testing

Monster Event Hire delivers professional inflatable inspections, PIPA inspections, and inflatable repairs for all types of inflatables, including:

  • Bouncy castles & inflatable slides
  • Obstacle courses & fun runs
  • Bouncing pillows & assault courses

We are an accredited RPII and PIPA inspection body, providing reliable inflatable inspections to ensure your equipment meets the latest safety standards.

PIPA Inspections for Inflatable Play

The PIPA inspection scheme (Portable Inflatable Play Association) sets safety standards for inflatables and is supported by the Health and Safety Executive (HSE).

Our team performs annual PIPA inspections for bouncy castles, inflatable slides, obstacle courses, and fun runs, following BS EN 14960:2019, the European standard for inflatable play equipment.

We carry out inflatable inspections on-site at your venue or at our Havant (Hampshire) base, covering Hampshire, Surrey, and Sussex, including schools, leisure centres, and MOD sites.

Note: Full payment applies for missed appointments or slots.

We also provide on-site inflatable repairs, including split seams, broken anchor points, or blown beds, to keep your inflatables safe and ready for play.

Rpii Operating & Attendant Courses

Our RPII courses train operators and attendants to handle inflatables safely. After completing the course, participants join the RPII Register of Safe Operators and Attendants for 3 years, guaranteeing professional, safe inflatable operation.

Non-PIPA Inflatable Inspections

We inspect non-PIPA EN14960-compliant inflatables, including:

  • Human table footballs
  • Bungee runs & eliminators
  • Ball pools
  • Rodeo bulls & surf simulators
  • Boxing rings & gladiator duels

Bungee Run Inspections

The BS EN 149604:2025 standard sets strict safety rules for bungee runs, covering minimum height, maximum weight, and user safety.

Other Non-Ride-On Inflatable Inspections

We issue PIPA tags for:

  • Football shootouts
  • Axe-throwing inflatables
  • Basketball hoops
  • Archery ranges

Bouncing Pillow Inspections

We perform bouncing pillow inspections under BS EN 14960-2:2019, ensuring your inflatable pillows meet European safety standards.
