The Green Giant Assault course

🎪 Inflatable Obstacle Course Hire – 70ft x 18ft x 15ft

Take your event to the next level with our inflatable assault course. Perfect for family fun days, corporate events, school reward days, or community festivals, this obstacle course challenges all comers. Participants can race, climb, and slide their way to victory in a safe, cushioned environment.

The course features an amazing wide-run section, designed for multiple participants at once. Along the way, challengers navigate tunnels, dodge giant balls, tackle the “Biff n Bash,” and squeeze through the “Mangle.” Finally, they climb a 10ft platform and race down the mega slide to reach the finish line. It’s high-energy fun that keeps everyone engaged.

🎈 Key Features

  • Equipment dimensions: 70ft x 18ft x 15ft high
  • Powered by 2 x standard 13amp plugs
  • Full drivable access required for delivery and setup
  • Suitable for indoor or outdoor use (space permitting)

🎉 Perfect For All Events
This inflatable obstacle course is ideal for:

  • Family fun days and community festivals
  • Corporate team-building challenges and staff events
  • School reward days or sports activity days
  • Any event looking to add a high-energy, competitive attraction

💡 Why Choose Our Obstacle Course?
It’s designed for fun, safe competition, and exciting challenges. Participants of all ages can enjoy climbing, crawling, dodging, and sliding in a thrilling environment. The durable, high-quality design ensures a safe experience while providing endless entertainment. In addition, the large 70ft layout makes it a standout feature that draws attention and keeps guests entertained.
