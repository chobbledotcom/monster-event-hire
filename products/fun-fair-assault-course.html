Fun Fair Assault Course – Inflatable Hire

Bring excitement and laughter to your event with our Fun Fair Assault Course! Designed to focus on fun and entertainment rather than competition, this inflatable obstacle course is perfect for birthday parties, school events, family fun days, community festivals, and corporate fun days. Suitable for children, teens, and adults, it delivers action-packed enjoyment for everyone.

Features

  • Dimensions: 50ft long × 12ft wide × 13ft high
  • Multiple inflatable obstacles including jump sections, squeeze tunnels, and slides
  • Fun fair-themed design with playful “bish and bash” obstacles
  • Suitable for kids and adults looking for active entertainment
  • Requires 2 x standard 13amp plugs for easy setup
  • Operated by trained staff to ensure safe and smooth gameplay

How It Works

Participants start at the run section and navigate through a variety of obstacles. Jump over hurdles, squeeze through tunnels, and dodge the playful “bish and bash” elements before finishing with an exhilarating slide. This fun-focused inflatable course encourages movement, laughter, and interactive play, making it ideal for events where fun is the priority.

Perfect for Any Event

The Fun Fair Assault Course is suitable for:

  • Birthday parties – A highlight attraction for kids and adults
  • School and college events – Safe, supervised, and engaging
  • Corporate fun days and team-building events – Lighthearted activity for colleagues
  • Community events, fairs, and festivals – Eye-catching inflatable entertainment

This inflatable obstacle course combines fun, excitement, and interactive challenges in one action-packed experience. It’s the perfect way to entertain crowds, encourage teamwork, and create memorable moments for your event.
