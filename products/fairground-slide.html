Fairground Slide

🎪 Classic Fairground Slide Hire – Hampshire & Surrey

Take sliding fun back to its roots with our Classic Fairground Slide, a timeless inflatable that brings all the excitement of a traditional funfair to your event. With its bright colours and nostalgic design, this slide is a fantastic centrepiece that instantly attracts attention and keeps guests entertained for hours.

Perfect for a wide range of occasions, this inflatable slide is suitable for both children and adults, making it a versatile addition to birthday parties, weddings, school fairs, community events, and corporate fun days. Its classic styling pairs perfectly with other fairground-themed attractions, helping you create a complete carnival atmosphere.

🎢 Key Features

  • Equipment size: 21ft (L) x 18ft (W) x 18ft (H)
  • Suitable for adults and children
  • Eye-catching classic fairground theme
  • High slide for added excitement and visibility
  • Ideal as a standalone attraction or part of a funfair setup

🎉 Perfect For

This Classic Fairground Slide is a popular choice for:

  • Birthday parties
  • School fetes and fun days
  • Weddings and family celebrations
  • Community festivals
  • Corporate and team-building events

Its height and bold design make it a real focal point, drawing guests in and creating a fun, energetic atmosphere.

📍 Space Requirements

Due to its 18ft height, this slide is best suited for outdoor events or large indoor venues with sufficient ceiling clearance. It’s ideal for fields, event spaces, and larger gardens.

🚚 Delivery & Setup

We offer professional delivery, full setup, and collection across Hampshire & Surrey, ensuring everything runs smoothly so you can focus on enjoying your event.

💡 Why Choose This Slide?

Our Classic Fairground Slide combines nostalgic charm with exciting gameplay, making it a must-have for anyone looking to recreate a traditional fairground experience. It’s simple, fun, and suitable for all ages—guaranteed to be a crowd favourite.
