Human Table Football (Ropes)

Human Table Football Ropes version .

Human table football ropes is a outrageously hilarious game of 5-a-side Football with a difference!

How did you get roped in to this? Full size human table football rope version, get strapped into the football table with ropes rather than bars.

Participants are strapped into place,  which only allow side-to-side movement and attempt to play football you then have to rely on teamwork skills to win.

Dimensions: 40ft x 20ft

Excellent for team building exercises and getting the energy levels up or just a simple game of friendly football!

  • Power: 1 x Standard 13amp plug (normal/domestic power point)
