85ft Jungle Assault Course Hire – Inflatable Obstacle Fun

Take your event to the next level with our Assault Course! Perfect for birthday parties, corporate team-building events, school sports days, university events, and community festivals, this inflatable obstacle course combines fun, fitness, and friendly competition. With challenging obstacles, jumps, and mega slides, it’s an exciting adventure suitable for both children and adults.

Features

  • Dimensions: 85ft long × 12ft wide × 15ft high – a visually impressive centerpiece for any event
  • Includes multiple obstacles, climbing areas, and mega slides
  • Suitable for children and adults, making it perfect for family-friendly events
  • Requires 3 × standard 13amp plugs for power
  • Safe and fully supervised when staffed by trained personnel
  • Ideal for indoor or outdoor events

How It Works

Participants race through the course, navigating obstacle-laden sections, jumping platforms, and climbing areas before finishing with a thrilling slide or two. The inflatable is designed to challenge agility, balance, and speed while ensuring safety and fun. With friendly competition encouraged, everyone gets a chance to test their skills and enjoy the adrenaline rush.

Perfect for Any Event

The 85ft Jungle Assault Course is ideal for:

  • Corporate events and team-building days – Promote collaboration and active engagement
  • Birthday parties – Fun, interactive entertainment for kids and adults
  • School and university activities – Safe, exciting, and energetic challenge for students
  • Community festivals and large-scale events – A visually impressive crowd-puller
