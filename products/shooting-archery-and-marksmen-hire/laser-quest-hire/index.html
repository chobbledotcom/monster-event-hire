Laser quest Hire

🔫 Laser Quest Hire – Hampshire, Surrey, Sussex & London

Bring fast-paced Laser Quest action to your event with Monster Event Hire! Whether it’s a birthday party, school fun day, corporate team-building event, or festival, players use infrared laser guns to tag opponents in safe, exciting, and fully supervised games. Plus, we cover Hampshire, Surrey, Sussex, and London, so wherever your event is, we can bring the ultimate laser tag experience directly to your venue.

🎮 How Laser Quest Works

Laser Quest is simple, fun, and action-packed. First, players compete in an arena using infrared laser guns. Next, each gun features built-in sensors, colored team lights, and score displays, which makes gameplay engaging and competitive. Additionally, up to 10 players (or more with multiple arenas) can compete simultaneously.

  • Games can be set up indoors or outdoors depending on your space.

  • Optional smoke effects create a realistic atmosphere (outdoor use is weather-dependent).

  • Finally, each game is staff-operated to ensure smooth gameplay and fair play.

In other words, the goal is to hit opponents without being tagged yourself, making every round competitive, fun, and full of adrenaline!

🌟 Safe & Supervised

Safety is our top priority. Therefore:

  • Fully staff-operated – a trained operator sets up and supervises every game.

  • No live projectiles – Laser Quest is 100% safe for all participants.

  • Suitable for children 6 years and older, as well as adults.

  • In addition, the arena is designed with clear boundaries and fast changeovers.

As a result, whether it’s a corporate event, birthday party, school fun day, or festival, you can trust Monster Event Hire to provide a safe, exciting experience for all players.

  • Outdoor height: 25 ft H required

  • Operational area: 40 ft × 40 ft

  • Power: 2 × standard 13 amp domestic sockets
