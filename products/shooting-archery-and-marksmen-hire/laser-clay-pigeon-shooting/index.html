Laser Clay Pigeon Shooting

🎯 Laser Clay Pigeon Shooting Hire – Monster Event Hire

Bring safe, competitive excitement to your event with Laser Clay Pigeon Shooting Hire! This fun, interactive attraction uses realistic infra‑red laser shotguns and automatic clay launchers to deliver a thrilling clay shooting experience without live ammunition. It’s perfect for birthday parties, corporate team building, school fun days, festivals, and weddings across Hampshire, Surrey, Sussex & London — guaranteed to entertain guests of all ages.

🎯 How Laser Clay Pigeon Shooting Works

Our laser clay pigeon shooting system uses the latest MK3 infra‑red technology to simulate real clay pigeon shooting:

  • Players use realistic shotgun replicas that fire infra‑red lasers

  • When the trigger is pulled, the scoreboard emits a “BANG” sound

  • Hitting a target triggers the sound of a breaking clay and records the score

  • Each session challenges players to hit as many clays as possible within a set round

  • Up to 5 players can compete at once, making it ideal for friendly competition

This makes the game engaging, interactive, and thrilling, with excitement for participants and spectators alike.

🏆 Fun for All Skill Levels

Whether your guests are beginners or experienced, Laser Clay Pigeon Shooting is designed to be accessible and entertaining:

  • Multiple launch angles and speeds keep players challenged

  • Adjustable game difficulty works for children and adults

  • Players can track their scores on a large digital display

  • Games can run indoors or outdoors, day or night

In other words, this activity combines realistic gameplay with safe and fun mechanics for everyone.

🛡️ Safe & Supervised Entertainment

We take safety seriously at every event:

  • Deactivated guns — no live ammunition, completely safe

  • Protective barriers keep flying targets away from participants

  • Fully operable by trained staff who manage each session

  • Adjustable sound levels and no physical recoil for comfort

  • Comes with £10 million Public Liability Insurance

This setup ensures your guests enjoy the game in a controlled, secure environment.

📍 Perfect for Many Events

Laser Clay Pigeon Shooting adds value and excitement to a wide range of occasions:

  • Birthday Parties – competitive fun for friends and family

  • Corporate Events & Team Building – build morale with challenge games

  • School Fun Days – safe, engaging activity for students

  • Festivals & Weddings – unique entertainment option

  • Outdoor & Indoor Gatherings – versatile setup

Plus, you can combine it with other attractions like archery hire, inflatables, or photo booths for a bigger event experience.

📐 Equipment & Requirements

  • Minimum area required: 30 m × 10 m (98 ft × 33 ft)

  • Fully staff‑supervised setup included

  • Suitable for indoor and outdoor events

  • Power not typically required unless using additional effects

  • Players must be able to get on/off the station unaided

We’ll take care of delivery, setup, supervision, and takedown, so you can focus on enjoying the event.

  • Hampshire

Wherever your event is — a sports field, village hall, festival site, or private garden — we’ll bring the equipment to you.
