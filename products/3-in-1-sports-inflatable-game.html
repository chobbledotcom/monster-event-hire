3 in 1 Sports Inflatable Game – Event Hire

Bring fun, competition, and active entertainment to your event. This versatile inflatable sports game is perfect for birthday parties, school fetes, carnivals, corporate fun days, and family events. It lets players test their sporting skills, challenge friends, and enjoy a range of sports activities in one exciting inflatable.

Features

  • Three sports in one inflatable: Baseball batting, basketball shooting, and hand-eye coordination darts
  • Dimensions: 18ft long × 10ft wide × 13ft high
  • Suitable for children, teens, and adults
  • Powered by 1 x standard 13amp plug for easy setup
  • Fully staffed by trained personnel for safe and smooth operation

How It Works

Players can rotate between the three challenges to test their sporting prowess:

  1. Baseball Batting – See how well you can hit the ball and show off your batting skills
  2. Basketball Shooting – Score points by shooting hoops in a mini basketball challenge
  3. Hand-Eye Coordination Darts – Test precision and focus with this fun inflatable darts game

The 3 in 1 Sports Inflatable Game provides fast-paced, competitive fun for groups of all ages. Its colorful, eye-catching design ensures it will stand out at any event, drawing crowds and keeping participants entertained for hours.
