50ft Army Assault Course – Inflatable Obstacle Challenge

Take on the ultimate inflatable challenge with our 50ft Army Assault Course! This popular inflatable obstacle course is perfect for birthday parties, school events, family fun days, corporate team-building, and community festivals. Designed for children, teens, and adults, it’s a thrilling, active experience that tests your agility, balance, and determination.

Features of the 50ft Army Assault Course

  • Dimensions: 50ft long × 12ft wide × 12ft high
  • Multiple inflatable obstacles including tunnels, slip-and-slide, and climbing challenges
  • Fun, interactive army-themed design with “biff and bash” soldiers along the course
  • Suitable for kids and adults who love an energetic challenge
  • Requires 2 x standard 13amp plugs for easy setup
  • Operated by trained staff for safe and smooth gameplay

How It Works

Enter through the letterbox opening and navigate your way through exciting obstacles. Dodge the “biff and bash” soldiers, squeeze through mangles, and tackle the up-and-down slip-and-slide to reach the finish line. The 50ft Army Assault Course is designed to push your limits while delivering maximum fun.

Perfect for Any Event

This inflatable army obstacle course is ideal for:

  • Birthday parties – A thrilling activity for kids and adults alike
  • School and college events – Fun, safe, and supervised competition
  • Corporate team-building – Encourages teamwork, stamina, and friendly rivalry
  • Community festivals and fairs – A standout attraction that draws crowds

The 50ft Army Assault Course combines action, excitement, and interactive fun in one incredible inflatable. Test your speed, skill, and stamina and see if you have what it takes to conquer this inflatable army challenge.
