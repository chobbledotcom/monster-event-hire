  ## Green Screen Photo Booth Hire
  Bring your event to life with our **Green Screen Photo Booth**! Perfect for **parties, corporate events, weddings, and themed celebrations**, this interactive photo booth lets your guests step into a world of fun and creativity.
  Guests sit against a **green backdrop**, and using **chroma-key technology**, the green screen is replaced with any of **up to seven different backgrounds**: Disco, Beach, Hollywood, Around the World, Underwater, Mystery, and Classic.
  The **Mystery button** can be customised with **four themed images** to suit your event, such as Christmas, Halloween, or an 80’s night.
  While your guests strike poses and have fun (what happens in the booth stays in the booth!), the **party organiser receives a USB stick** containing all the photos and videos from the event. Guests also get **printouts of their photos on the night**.
  ### Key Features
  -   **Unlimited photos** during hire (approx. 500 photos in 3 hours)
      
  -   **4 photos per session** printed on the night
      
  -   **USB stick** with all photos and videos supplied on the night
      
  -   **Booth attendant** included to assist and manage the booth
      
  -   **Free prop box** with hats, masks, glasses, and novelty items
      
  -   **Free basic customisation** of prints with company or event name
      
  -   **Setup and removal** included
      
  ### Requirements
  -   **Indoor or marquee use only**
      
  -   **Flat, level space required:** 2.3m wide × 1.25m deep × 2.1m high
