Football Speed Cage

Football Speed Cage Hire – Test Your Shot and Power

Bring action-packed sports fun to your next event with our Football Speed Cage hire! Whether you’re hosting a birthday party, corporate team-building day, school sports day, university event, or community festival, this inflatable challenge lets players test their speed, accuracy, and power in an interactive and exciting environment. With real-time feedback, it guarantees fun for all ages and abilities.

Features of the Football Speed Cage

  • Compact yet high-impact: 13ft × 13ft × 13ft, perfect for any venue
  • Instant speed feedback: The LED display shows the speed of each shot in MPH with an accuracy of ±1 mph
  • Multi-sport compatibility: Works with footballs, rugby balls, handballs, basketballs, golf balls, and more
  • Highly addictive: Participants keep coming back for “just one more shot”
  • Versatile hire options: Available alone or as part of a sports-themed event package
  • Indoor or outdoor use: Set up easily and safely
  • Fully staffed: Ensures smooth operation and participant safety

How It Works

Players kick, throw, or serve a ball into the Football Speed Cage. Instantly, the LED display shows the speed, adding excitement and encouraging friendly competition. Because it combines action, challenge, and instant feedback, the Speed Cage draws attention, engages participants, and keeps spectators entertained.

Why It’s Perfect for Your Event

The Football Speed Cage works wonderfully for:

  • Corporate events and team-building days – Boost engagement and teamwork
  • Birthday parties – Keep kids, teens, and adults active and entertained
  • School and university sports days – Safe, fun, and interactive
  • Community festivals and sports-themed events – A true crowd-puller

By combining high visual impact, competitive gameplay, and accessibility, the Football Speed Cage provides a memorable, interactive experience for everyone. Add it to your event hire lineup and watch guests compete, laugh, and enjoy themselves throughout the day.
