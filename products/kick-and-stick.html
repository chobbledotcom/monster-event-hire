Kick N Stick foot darts

Double Sided Kick N Stick foot darts !

Introducing our Double Sided Giant Kick N Stick foot darts, this is a fun and interactive game for everyone.

A combination of the beautiful game we call football and a British pub classic.

This Giant inflatable, allows you and your users to kick the Velcro footballs onto the giant dart board, trying to get that magic ” bulls eye ” or on the other side of the inflatable play Kick N Stick you’ll have endless fun and games as your players try to kick the balls to score the most points.

Is your aim good ? let’s find out ! This mash-up of football and darts involves an inflatable dartboard, Velcro footballs and a big hoop on a stick to retrieve the ball.

Football fans will love it as it not only tests your skills but you can compete against your friends or colleagues !

This is a true test of balance, skill, stamina and sheer determination to get that winning score or Bulls-eye.

This item is a perfect add-on to any family fun day, sports club, team building and universities.
