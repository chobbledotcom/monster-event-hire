Rock ‘n’ Roller Gladiator Joust Hire in Hampshire & Surrey

The Ultimate Inflatable Gladiator Duel with a Rocking Twist

Looking for a unique inflatable hire in Hampshire or Surrey that guarantees laughs, competition, and excitement? The Rock ‘n’ Roller Gladiator Joust takes the classic gladiator duel and upgrades it with a moving, rocking base that challenges balance as much as strength.

Perfect for parties, corporate events, school fun days, weddings, and festivals, this inflatable is a real crowd-puller.

What Makes the Rock ‘n’ Roller Different?

Unlike standard gladiator inflatables, this version features a shared rotating and rocking podium.

Two players face off using padded jousting poles—but as the base shifts beneath them, staying upright becomes the real challenge!

👉 It’s not just about strength—it’s about balance, timing, and strategy.
