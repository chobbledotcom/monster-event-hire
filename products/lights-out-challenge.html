Lights Out Challenge

🎮 Lights Out Challenge Hire – Test Your Speed and Reactions

Take on the Lights Out Challenge and see who has the fastest reactions! Compete against an opponent in 30 or 60-second games to see who can turn off the most lights. It’s a thrilling test of hand-eye coordination, speed, and focus.

With three different levels of increasing speed, the challenge ramps up as players improve. It’s suitable for children and adults aged 6+, making it perfect for mixed groups and family-friendly competitions.

Check Out our video!

 

 

🎈 Key Features

  • Maximum users: 2 at a time
  • Suitable for indoor or marquee use only
  • Requires 13amp 240V power supply
  • Adjustable game durations: 30 or 60 seconds
  • Three speed levels for increasing challenge

🎉 Perfect For All Events
The Lights Out Challenge is ideal for:

  • Corporate events and team-building exercises
  • Birthday parties and house parties
  • Product launches and promotional events
  • University events, office parties, and fun competitions

💡 Why Choose Lights Out Challenge?
This interactive game is fast-paced, competitive, and highly engaging. It tests reflexes, coordination, and focus, while providing hours of fun and entertainment. Whether used for friendly competition or structured team challenges, it’s a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that keeps participants and spectators involved.
