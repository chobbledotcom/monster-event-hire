The Magic Selfie Mirror is an interactive photo station that lets guests take high‑quality photos in a fun and engaging way. Its sleek mirrored design shows entertaining animations, prompts for poses, and delivers instant keepsake prints.

In other words, it’s more than a photo booth — it’s a memorable experience that guests can’t stop talking about.

🎉 How It Works

Using the Magic Selfie Mirror is easy and fun for everyone:

  1. Guests approach the mirror and follow the on‑screen prompts

  2. Strike a pose with props or solo

  3. The mirror counts down and takes multiple photos

  4. Guests receive instant prints to take home

  5. Optional digital sharing lets guests email or text their photos
