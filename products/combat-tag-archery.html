Combat Tag Archery Hire – Team Building & Corporate Events

Bring adrenaline, fun, and friendly competition to your next event with Combat Tag Archery hire! Also known as battle archery or tag archery, this fast-paced game combines elements of dodgeball, Nerf battles, and paintball into a safe, competitive archery experience. Perfect for corporate team-building, birthday parties, school activities, and group events, it’s guaranteed to keep participants engaged and entertained.

How Combat Tag Archery Works

Players are divided into two teams of five and armed with specialist archery bows and safe foam-tipped arrows. The aim is to hit members of the opposing team while also targeting the centre scoreboard. Teams compete to eliminate their opponents first, creating a fast-paced and exhilarating experience.

With trained staff supervising, Combat Tag Archery ensures safe and smooth gameplay for all participants. All equipment comes with £10 million public liability insurance, giving you peace of mind while everyone enjoys the game.

Key Features of Combat Tag Archery

  • Safe foam-tipped arrows – suitable for kids and adults
  • Team sizes: 5 players per team
  • Fast-paced, competitive gameplay for all skill levels
  • Perfect for corporate team-building, birthday parties, college events, and group challenges
  • Fully operated by trained staff
  • Power requirement: 1 × standard 13amp domestic plug
