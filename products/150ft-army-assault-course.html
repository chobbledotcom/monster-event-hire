150ft Army Assault Course – Inflatable Obstacle Challenge

Take on the ultimate test of strength, speed, and agility with our 150ft Army Assault Course – the largest inflatable army obstacle course available! Stretching a massive 150 feet in length, this inflatable course is designed to challenge both children and adults while providing endless fun and excitement for participants and spectators alike. Perfect for birthday parties, school events, corporate team-building, community festivals, and large outdoor events, this inflatable course guarantees unforgettable entertainment.

Features of the 150ft Army Assault Course

  • Massive 150ft length packed with multiple obstacles
  • Challenging walls, slides, tunnels, and runways
  • Suitable for kids, teens, and adults
  • Safe, durable, and fully staffed for smooth operation
  • Perfect for team-building events, birthday parties, school challenges, corporate fun days, and community events

How the Course Works

Participants navigate a series of obstacles that test strength, stamina, balance, and coordination. Climb walls, slide down slides, sprint across runways, and crawl through tunnels as you race to the finish line. The course encourages friendly competition, teamwork, and determination, making it ideal for team-building exercises and fun competitions. Spectators will enjoy watching participants tackle each obstacle, making it a highlight of any event.

Dimensions & Setup

  • Size: 150ft long × 12ft wide × 15ft high
  • Power: Requires 6 x standard 13amp plugs (normal/domestic power points)
  • Suitable for outdoor venues and large indoor spaces
  • Fully operated by trained staff for safety and efficient use
