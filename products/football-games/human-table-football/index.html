Human Table Football

Deluxe Human Table Football – Event Hire

Bring the excitement of football to life with our Deluxe Human Table Football – a giant inflatable version of the classic table-top game. Perfect for team-building, corporate events, school fun days, and parties, this game delivers high-energy fun for players and spectators alike.

Features of Deluxe Human Table Football

  • Life-sized 10-player game: Two 5-a-side teams compete head-to-head
  • Authentic gameplay: Players use bars, not ropes, for a realistic football experience
  • Spectator-friendly: Watch the action as players move left and right, strategizing to score goals
  • Test of skill and teamwork: Balance, timing, stamina, and coordination are essential to win

Perfect for Any Event

Human Table Football adds excitement to a wide range of events:

  • Schools & Colleges – Fun reward at the end of term or part of sports programs
  • Scouts, Guides, and Youth Groups – Engaging team-based activity
  • Corporate Team Building – Promote cooperation, communication, and healthy competition
  • Parties & Festivals – Indoor or outdoor entertainment all year round

Dimensions & Setup

  • Size: 50ft x 30ft
  • Players: Up to 10 at a time
  • Operated by fully trained staff
  • Powered by 1 x standard 13amp plug
  • Covered by £10 Million Public Liability Insurance
