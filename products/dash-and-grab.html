      Dash N Grab Inflatable Hire in Hampshire & Surrey 🎯

      Giant Hungry Hippos Style Game – 8 Player Interactive Fun

      Ready to bring back a childhood classic with a competitive twist?

      Our Dash N Grab inflatable hire in Hampshire & Surrey is inspired by the iconic Hungry Hungry Hippos—but supersized into an 8-player, high-energy inflatable challenge!

      Players become the “hippos” as they race, grab, and compete to collect the most balls from the centre—while being pulled back by a bungee cord!

      How Dash N Grab Works

      It’s simple, chaotic, and incredibly fun:

      1. Up to 8 players are attached to bungee cords

      2. Dash to the centre of the inflatable arena

      3. Grab as many balls as possible

      4. Fight against the pull of the bungee as you return

      5. Deposit balls into your net

      👉 The player with the most balls at the end wins!

      • 👨‍🔧 Staff Included

        A trained team member is included to run and manage the game, ensuring everything operates safely and smoothly.

        Dimensions & Requirements

        • Size: 12m x 12m (39ft x 39ft)

        • Height: 2.6m (9ft)

        • Users: Up to 8 players at once

        • Age Range: 10 years+

        • Power: 2 x standard 13amp plugs

        • Access: Full drive-on access required

        👉 Suitable for both indoor and outdoor use (space permitting).
