Features of the Gold Cup Horse Racing Game

  • Authentic grass-textured racecourse for realistic play
  • Four colour-coded hobby-horses (blue, yellow, red, green)
  • Realistic horse racing commentary to enhance the experience
  • Compact, portable, and easy to transport

How to Play

Each player straddles their hobby horse, holding the handles at the horse’s head. Players simulate a galloping motion – the faster you gallop, the faster your horse moves along the racecourse.

The first horse to reach the finishing post wins! The winning lane lights up, and commentary announces the horse’s colour and name. After each race, horses return to the starting posts, ready for the next game.

The game comes self-contained in a durable industrial flight case. Its dimensions:
