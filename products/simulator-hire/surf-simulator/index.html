🌊 How the Surf Simulator Works

The Surf Simulator is a mobile mechanical surfboard that mimics the movement of real ocean waves. Guests can:

  • Balance and ride on a motorized board

  • Experience safe, controlled wave movements

  • Compete for longest ride or best tricks

  • Enjoy instant thrills suitable for beginners and advanced riders alike

This makes it ideal for interactive competitions, party challenges, or just pure fun.

🌟 Features & Safety

  • Staff-operated for full supervision and safety

  • Inflatable landing area to ensure soft landings

  • Suitable for indoor or outdoor events

  • Adjustable difficulty for all ages and skill levels

  • Comes with £10 million public liability insurance for peace of mind
